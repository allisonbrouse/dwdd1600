Event Size

Splash Haven is the perfect place for all parties both big and small! Between our Lazy River, Mega Pool, Splash Slide Tower, and Kiddie Palace, your group will have no shortage of activities to enjoy. Our property can be rented for groups up to 5,000 guests, with many booking options. Pricing depends on group size.

FAQ

How far in advance should I book my event?
Splash Haven requires events be booked at least 2 weeks in advance, but we recommend booking a month before your desired date for a higher chance of getting the exact date wanted.
How do I schedule an event?
To schedule an event, email or call our offices to secure your date and services.

Can I reschedule/cancel an event I have already booked?
Events can be rescheduled/canceled within two weeks prior to the event date to receive a full refund.
